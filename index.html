Mara Manyike

South African Gospel Singer

Inspiring Gospel Music in Xitsonga, Isizulu, and Sotho

Mara Manyike is a renowned South African gospel singer, celebrated for her soulful voice and spiritual guidance. As an evangelist in prophetic churches and a pastor in non-prophetic churches, she touches hearts with her multilingual songs.

About Mara

Mara Manyike's journey in gospel music started in her home language, Xitsonga, and expanded to include Isizulu and Sotho. Her dedication to spreading the word of God through music has made her a beloved figure in many communities. Her performances are known for their powerful message and uplifting energy.
